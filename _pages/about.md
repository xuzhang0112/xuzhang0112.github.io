I am a first-year PhD student at the BME School of [USTC](https://en.ustc.edu.cn), supervised by Prof. [S. Kevin Zhou](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=8eNm2GMAAAAJ&hl=en). I received my bachelor's degree from [SCU](https://eng.suda.edu.cn) in 2023, tutored by Prof. [Dehui Xiang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=egSgzRQAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao).

I'm currently focusing on long text understanding and its biomedical applications (Medical coding and diagnosis).


## Education

- **2023-present**: EE, PhD student, USTC
- **2019-2023**: EE, Bachelor, SCU
